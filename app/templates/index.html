About Jazzy's Family Creations

{% endcomment %}

Jazzy's Family Creations is a startup business where I, Jazzy Harris, decorate and set up events for customers. The events include but are not limited to: Anniversary's, Weddings, Birthdays, Celebrations, and general Parties. You the customer has to choose the location and book it, you get to choose the theme of your event, and choose the number of people who are going to show up.
