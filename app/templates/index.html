About Jazzy's Family Creations

{% endcomment %}

Jazzy's Family Creations is a startup business where I, Jazzy Harris, decorate and set up events for customers. I am only able to do one event per day, on any Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. The events include but are not limited to: Anniversary's, Weddings, Birthdays, Celebrations, and general Parties. You the customer has to choose the location and book it, you get to choose the theme of your event, and choose the number of people who are going to show up.

The customer gets to choose the package for the event they're planning. I have the Basic Package which is $275, the Premium Package which is $375, and the Gold Package which is $475. Prices may differ depending on the size of the venue and the amount of people going to attend. A deposit is required to book your event, it will be 1/2 of the main price. The full price is acceptable as well. Two weeks after your event the second half of the price is reqired to be payed.

In order to book your event please make your way to the calendar tab where you can see what days are avaliable and what days are booked. To schedule an event, I have to be informed and paid the deposit 2 weeks in advance of the event. To find out what all is included in each package, please make your way through the tabs to the package tab and view what comes with each package.
